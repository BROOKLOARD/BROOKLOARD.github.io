About Me

I am a highly results-driven professional with extensive project management experience. Based in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, I currently serve as the Project Management Office Manager at IE Network Solutions, where I lead and oversee the entire project portfolio across departments.

I hold an MBA with a 3.8 GPA from Lunar International College and a BSc in Construction Technology and Management from Hawassa University. I am a Project Management Professional (PMP) certified and also hold the Certified Associate in Project Management (CAPM) certification.

Throughout my career, I have successfully managed projects worth millions of dollars for prestigious clients including the Ministry of Innovation and Technology, Ethiopia Pharmaceuticals Supply Service, United Nations Economic Commission for Africa (UNECA), and the Office of the Federal Auditor General.

I am known for my keen analytical abilities, meticulous attention to detail, and capacity to deliver projects ahead of schedule—most notably completing a $4.72 million project 76 days early.
